INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat a pan over low heat.
Separate the {1:Egg}s into whites and yolks. Place the whites in a bowl and set the yolks aside.
Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
Mix the yolks, {1:Milk}, {1:Sugar}, {1:Flour}, and {1:Vanilla Extract} into the egg whites.
Portion the batter into the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side on low heat.

Flip the pancakes when bubbles form on the surface.
